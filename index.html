About

Who I Am.

I'm Erico, an apprentice cybersecurity engineer who believes the best defense starts with understanding the offense. My work sits at the intersection of digital forensics, adversarial machine learning and security tooling.

I build open-source tools that make forensic analysis faster, AI models more robust, and incident response more systematic. From LLM-assisted forensics to adversarial hardening, I explore how AI can both attack and defend.
